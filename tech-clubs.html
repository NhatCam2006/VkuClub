VKU IT Club

A community for IT enthusiasts to learn, share knowledge, and develop technical skills.

The VKU IT Club is a vibrant community of technology enthusiasts dedicated to exploring and mastering various aspects of information technology. Our club provides a platform for students to enhance their technical skills, collaborate on innovative projects, and stay updated with the latest trends in the tech industry.

Founded: 2020
Meetings: Every Wednesday, 5:00 PM - 7:00 PM
Location: Building A, Room 305
Email: itclub@vku.udn.vn

VKU AI Research Club

Exploring artificial intelligence, machine learning, and their applications in solving real-world problems.

The VKU AI Research Club is dedicated to exploring the fascinating world of artificial intelligence and machine learning. Our members work on cutting-edge research projects, develop AI applications, and participate in competitions to solve real-world problems using AI technologies.

Founded: 2021
Meetings: Every Tuesday, 6:00 PM - 8:00 PM
Location: Building A, AI Lab, Room 401
Email: aiclub@vku.udn.vn

VKU Game Development Club

Creating interactive games and exploring game design, development, and programming.

VKU Game Development Club brings together students passionate about game design and development. We explore various aspects of game creation including programming, art design, storytelling, and sound engineering. Our members collaborate on game projects, participate in game jams, and learn industry-standard tools and techniques.

Founded: 2021
Meetings: Every Thursday, 5:30 PM - 8:30 PM
Location: Building C, Game Lab, Room 205
