Community Service Clubs

Explore VKU's community service clubs where social responsibility, volunteerism, and making a difference are prioritized

VKU Volunteer Club

Make a positive impact in the community through various volunteer and charity initiatives.

VKU Volunteer Club is dedicated to making a positive impact in our community through service and outreach programs. Our members engage in various charitable activities including educational support for underprivileged children, environmental conservation efforts, blood donation drives, and disaster relief initiatives. We believe in the power of collective action and strive to instill a sense of social responsibility among VKU students.

Founded: 2020
Meetings: Every second Saturday, 9:00 AM - 11:00 AM
Location: Building B, Room 101
Email: volunteerclub@vku.udn.vn

VKU Green Club

Promoting environmental awareness and sustainability through campus and community initiatives.

VKU Green Club is committed to promoting environmental awareness, sustainability, and eco-friendly practices within the university and broader community. Our activities include campus greening projects, waste reduction campaigns, environmental education workshops, and conservation initiatives. We collaborate with local environmental organizations and government agencies to implement sustainable solutions and raise awareness about pressing environmental issues.

Founded: 2021
Meetings: Every Friday, 2:00 PM - 4:00 PM
Location: Building A, Room 202
Email: greenclub@vku.udn.vn
View Club Details Join This Club

VKU Mentorship Club

Supporting student success through peer mentoring, academic guidance, and personal development.

VKU Mentorship Club connects experienced students with newcomers to provide guidance, support, and resources for academic and personal success. Our mentors help first-year and international students navigate university life, develop effective study habits, and connect with campus resources. We organize workshops on time management, stress reduction, career planning, and other essential skills.

Founded: 2021
Meetings: Every Wednesday, 3:00 PM - 5:00 PM
Location: Building B, Room 205
Email: mentorclub@vku.udn.vn
