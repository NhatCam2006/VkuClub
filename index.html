About VKU

Vietnam-Korea University of Information and Communication Technology (VKU) is a public university established in 2020, located in the central city of Da Nang, Vietnam. It was founded with cooperation between Vietnam and South Korea.

VKU focuses on training high-quality human resources in Information Technology, Communication Technology, and related fields to meet the demands of the digital transformation era and Industry 4.0.

The university encourages students to participate in extracurricular activities through various clubs to develop soft skills, leadership, and teamwork abilities alongside their academic studies.
